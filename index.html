PROJECT 3

Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM)

I created a detailed AutoCAD model of a Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) machine. The model includes key components like the sensor, transmitter, and receiver/display unit. The sensor is designed to be placed under the skin for real-time glucose measurement, while the transmitter sends data wirelessly to the display unit. The design focuses on compactness, ease of use, and clarity of component layout—ideal for understanding the structure and functionality of a CGM system.
